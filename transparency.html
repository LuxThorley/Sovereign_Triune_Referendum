Transparency principles

Publish aggregates, not identities. Transparency means clear methods and totals — not exposing individuals.
  • We publish: totals, rates, thresholds, method notes, Harmony Index snapshots.
  • We do not publish: raw submissions or personal identifiers.

Recommended counting method

  1. Collect JSON submissions via email or secure upload.
  2. Validate structure and window compatibility.
  3. Deduplicate using pseudonymous VoterID plus review.
  4. Compute totals and regional approval rates.
  5. Publish anonymised aggregates and update results.json.

Threshold reminders

  • Contest trigger: approval <70% OR ≥25% prefer contest OR Regent calls it.
  • Regional balance: approval ≥60% in ≥4 of 6 regions.

Admin: publish results on this site

  1. Edit results.json in the site root.
  2. Upload/replace it on the server.
  3. Refresh this page — it auto-renders your totals.
